I'm Marrisa, I am 14 years old, born on August 11th 2008 in Bangkok, Thailand, but I am Cambodian. I lived in Cambodia up until I was 3 years old and then my family and I moved to Auckland, New Zealand. It took me a while to adjust to New Zealand because everything and everyone is different here in New Zealand. I prefer living in Cambodia because of the culture there, but my parents moved here because the school opportunities are better. I'm currently a year 10 student at Botany downs secondary college and I graduate in 2026. this website was created for my 10DIT class in 2023.
